I am thrilled to share our latest exciting research — **Coupled Rigid-Block Analysis (CRA)** — published in the SCI journal [*Computer-Aided Design*](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cad.2022.103216).

CRA is a new method to measure structural stability statically and can be used to design complex discrete-element assemblies. It can be applied to many research fields like designing architectures, furniture, 3D puzzles, toys, or even robotic assembly planning. Please see our open-access paper for more details!

**[→ Read the open-access paper](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cad.2022.103216)**
