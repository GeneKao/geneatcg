Teaching assistant and mentor for a one-month MIT Design-Build Workshop 2019 in Shanghai. Robotic Force Printing is a joint workshop from MIT Architecture, ETH Zurich Block Research Group, and Tongji University. The workshop explores robotic additive manufacture and assembly of novel shell structures.
